What We Do

Six ways we help — always starting with the simplest one that works.

Product Strategy

We help you figure out what to build — and what not to. From roadmaps to requirements, we translate your mission into a clear product plan.

Implementation Consulting

Already have a tool in mind? We guide the rollout — vendor selection, configuration, training, and adoption — so your team gets real value from day one.

R&D & Custom Solutions

When off-the-shelf won't cut it, our engineering team steps in to design and build exactly what's needed — nothing more, nothing less.

Non-Profit Focus

We understand the constraints and aspirations of mission-driven work. Every engagement is shaped around your budget, your people, and your purpose.

Impact & Reporting

Funders want numbers. Your team wants clarity. We help you set up reporting and data practices that serve both — without drowning in dashboards.

Training & Adoption

A tool nobody uses is a tool nobody needed. We work alongside your team to make sure new processes actually stick — through hands-on training, documentation, and ongoing support.

Why Stargazer?

Too many non-profits get sold technology they don't need by consultants who don't understand their world. We started Stargazer Innovations because we believe mission-driven organizations deserve a partner who listens first and builds second.

Our default is simplicity. We lead with product consulting — understanding your goals, mapping your workflows, and finding the most straightforward path forward. We only bring in engineering when it's genuinely the right call. The result is less waste, less complexity, and more impact.

Listen first. Build second.
