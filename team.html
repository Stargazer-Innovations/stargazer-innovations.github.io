Meet the Team

Ed Koharik

Chief Executive Officer

Ed leads Stargazer's consulting practice and is usually the first person a client talks to. He's spent the previous three years as a product manager at Youth Villages, so he reads a mission-driven org's constraints before the engineering starts — the tight budget, the lean staff, the tool that has to keep working long after the project wraps. He also teaches innovation at Missouri S&T, where the whole subject is helping good ideas reach the people they're meant to serve. He works directly with each team to understand the real problem and map a practical path. His philosophy is simple: listen first, recommend only what's needed, and stay until it actually works.

Cooper Healy

Director of Research & Development

Cooper is the engineer Stargazer brings in when a project needs something built, not bought. He's worked across data, infrastructure, and product for over a decade — he founded the data department at a venture-backed startup, and earlier cut a company's cloud costs by 85% with a single redesign. For nonprofit work, he cares most about one thing: that what he builds is something your team can run on its own after the engagement ends — no vendor lock-in, no black boxes, nothing that needs a consultant on retainer to keep alive.
